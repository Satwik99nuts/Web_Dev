Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 ½ teaspoons of yeast
  • ½ cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup of butter
  • 4 ½ cups of flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Warm the milk until it is warm but not hot.
  2. Add the yeast and a pinch of sugar and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine the flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and milk mixture.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  6. Roll out the dough.
  7. Spread the filling on the dough.
  8. Roll up the dough and cut it into 12 pieces.
  9. Let the rolls rise for 30 minutes.
  10. Bake the rolls at 180°C for 25 minutes.
